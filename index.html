About Chittagong

Chittagong, officially known as Chattogram, is the second-largest city in Bangladesh and the country's main seaport. Nestled between the Bay of Bengal and the lush green hills, this vibrant city offers a unique blend of natural beauty, rich history, and bustling commerce.

Known as the "Port City, " Chittagong boasts stunning beaches, scenic hill districts, and a fascinating cultural heritage. From the serene Patenga Beach to the majestic Foy's Lake, the city is a treasure trove of experiences waiting to be discovered.

Whether you're seeking adventure, relaxation, or cultural immersion, Chittagong has something special for every traveler.
